Six Strings

1) Marcin  Patrzalek

In the list of top guitarists in the world. He has been on American Got Talent. He has performed from AGT’s Stage to basement and even in the elevator. G.O.A.T!

2) Somyajit Pyne.

The most underrated  fingerstyle guitarist. He has been playing guitar for seven years now.He is a self learnt guitarist and can play all styles from flamenco to fingerpicking. Not heard anyone better than him from India.

2 Rahul Das

Rahul is also amazing. Discovered him from His Shankar stotram video. He has also done collabration with Somyajit in the gulabi aankhien video whose arrangement was inspiried from the Sanam’s version.

3. Yash Garg

The version of Filal and Shayad by him are the best arrangements one can ever find on Youtube. Also, the visuals in his videos are on point.

4. Shrey Gupta.

@thatfingerstyleguitarist  Discovered him through the breathless fingerstyle cover(old one). He has also performed in Salim Merchant’s World Music  Day  video.

The best quartet so far.  they started a series of practising guitar and  now they have posted more than 500 videos. They also run Ny Guitar Academy. And they also have  second  Instagram Page called the  Rumbaflamenca.

Soothing solos.  Discovered him through his Jashne Bahara Reel. He also uploads tutorial to explain Indian classical guitar with the help  of a guitar which is rare.
